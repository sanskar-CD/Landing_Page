Google Analytics Events & Goals

Tracked Events

Events are user interactions such as clicks, scrolls, and form submissions.

Goals / Conversions

Goals represent important actions like sign-ups or contact form submissions.

Scroll Tracking

Scrolling can also be tracked as an engagement event.

Understanding Bot Traffic

Bot traffic refers to non-human visits generated by automated programs called bots.

Search Engine Bots

Examples: Googlebot, Bingbot. They crawl websites for indexing in search engines.

Malicious Bots

Used for scraping data, credential stuffing, DDoS attacks, or fake traffic generation.

Bot Traffic in Analytics

Bot traffic can inflate sessions, reduce conversion rates, and distort analytics reports.

What is a Proxy & How Bots Use It

A proxy server acts as an intermediary between a user (or bot) and the internet.

  • Hides the original IP address
  • Helps bypass geo-restrictions
  • Used by bots to rotate IPs and avoid detection

Bot services often use residential, datacenter, or mobile proxies to appear as legitimate users and generate traffic from different locations.
